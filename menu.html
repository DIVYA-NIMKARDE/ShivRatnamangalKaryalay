SPECIAL

Baingan Masala

This recipe consists of curry with the base of onion and tomato. Usually, this Baingan masala recipe also has desiccated coconut in the curry base.

Aloo mutter

Aloo mutter is made from potatoes and peas in a spiced creamy tomato based sauce. It is a vegetarian dish. The sauce is generally cooked with garlic, ginger, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, cumin seeds and other spices.

paneer

This dish consisting of peas and paneer in a tomato based sauce, spiced with garam masala. It is often served with rice and an Indian type of bread. This is very spicy & most special menu in our kitchen.

MAIN

Bhendi Fry

Bhendi Fry is stuffed with spice mix such as garam masala and other locally available ground spices.

Gulab jamun

Gulab jamun is a milk-solid-based sweet, a type of mithai, popular in India. When you first bite into a Gulab Jamun, you will find that it's incredibly sweet. They are a lovely dessert food in our Bureau. Basically, this may be one of these sweetest desserts you will try.

Rabri

Rabri is a sweet, condensed-milk-based dish, made by boiling milk on low heat for a long time until it becomes dense and changes its colour to off-white or pale yellow. Jaggery, spices, and nuts are added to it to give it flavor. It is chilled and served as dessert.

DESSERTS

Sweets, Barfi

It is a dense milk-based sweet from the Indian subcontinent, and a type of mithai. A few of the famous varieties of barfi include besan barfi, kaaju barfi, pista barfi, and sing barfi.

Pulav

It's delicious and stomach filling. This is a dish with healthy & all the right vitamins, nutrients and minerals. Made with aromatic long grains cooked with mixed healthy vegetables and spices.

Ice Cream

Ice cream is a sweetened frozen food. It may be made from dairy milk or cream and is flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit such as strawberries or peaches.
